VOSS AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. Introduction

VOSS AI is a private, internal application built and operated exclusively by Vossler Plumbing, Fort Worth, Texas. This application is not offered to the public. This privacy policy describes how data is handled within the application.

2. Data We Access

VOSS AI connects to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's official API to access Vossler Plumbing's own business data, including:

  • Company information
  • Customer names, contact details, and balances
  • Invoices and payment records
  • Financial reports (profit & loss, accounts receivable)

3. How Data Is Used

All data accessed through QuickBooks Online is used solely for Vossler Plumbing's internal business operations, including job costing, invoicing, accounts receivable management, and financial reporting.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is processed and stored locally on Vossler Plumbing's own hardware. No business or financial data is transmitted to third-party services, cloud storage, or external databases beyond Intuit's own APIs. OAuth tokens are stored locally with restricted file permissions.

5. Data Sharing

Vossler Plumbing does not sell, share, rent, or disclose any data accessed through this application to any third party.

6. Third-Party Services

The only external service VOSS AI connects to is QuickBooks Online (operated by Intuit Inc.), governed by Intuit's Privacy Statement.

7. Data Retention

Data accessed from QuickBooks Online is used in real time and is not permanently cached or archived beyond what is necessary for immediate business operations.

For questions about this privacy policy, contact Vossler Plumbing at vosslerplumbing817@gmail.com.
